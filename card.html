Moose

Moose can have antlers that span up to 5 feet and weigh over 65 pounds!
  • Scientific name: Alces alces (Al-ces al-ces)
  • Height: 4.6 - 6.9 ft.
  • Lifespan: 15 - 25 years
  • Habitat: Forested and snowy areas

Moose are the largest of all deer species. The males are easily recongnized by their huge antlers. A full grown male Moose can weigh over 1200 pounds! During the summer, Moose eat up to 75 pounds of assorted shurbs, woody plants, and aquatic vegetation. Moose can be found all over Canada and Alaska.
